# New Manhattan: The New Political & Tech Capital

Location & rationale
- 15–20 miles north along MetroNorth corridor: higher elevation, existing rail access.

Design features
- Elevated arcologies, EMP/geomagnetic-hardened infrastructure, microgrids, sovereign AI stacks.
- Distributed robotics manufacturing and data centers with air-gapped secure clusters.

Social & political structure
- Headquarters for corporations and tech-oligarchs.
- Real seat of power; elites live here rather than Old Manhattan.

Transit & symbolism
- MetroNorth becomes ceremonial link: trains ferry tourists to Old Manhattan while executives commute to New Manhattan.

Narrative use
- Contrast between curated Old Manhattan (symbolism) and functional New Manhattan (power).
- New Manhattan as a locus of contested tech-power (who controls hardened AI?).
