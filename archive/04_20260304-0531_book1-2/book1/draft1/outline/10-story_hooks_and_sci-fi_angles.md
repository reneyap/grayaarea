# Story Hooks & Sci‑Fi Angles

High-level motifs
- Body abandoned, brain preserved: Old Manhattan as a curated corpse.
- Technology as both salvation and catalyst for collapse (X20 resets AI; robots win the war but reshape society).

Possible scenes
- The X20 night: EMP-like sky, blackouts, PRIME awakening.
- MetroNorth ceremonial ride: tourists crossing into Old Manhattan.
- The vote night: Strategic Island Retraction Act passes amid protests and spectacle.
- Eli’s conversion: battlefield, operating theater, and the politics around cyborgs.

Philosophical threads
- Memory vs agency: cities and hosts preserving identity without living systems.
- Ownership of history: elite-curated narratives vs lived experience of displaced populations.

Writing tips
- Keep technical detail enough to anchor plausibility (sea-level numbers, geomagnetic terminology) but foreground human stakes.
- Use contrasts: preserved illusion vs visible decay; curated tourists vs scavengers beyond the wall.
