# Preservation Zone & Upper East Side Crescent

Core concept
- A curated preservation crescent protects Midtown, Grand Central, Central Park, Museum Mile and a symbolic Upper East Side strip.

Political mechanics
- Funded largely by ultra-wealthy trusts and federal heritage funds; framed as cultural preservation.
- UES is a symbol: preserved façades and private museums, not permanent elite residence.

Physical features
- Perimeter seawall(s), elevated causeways, climate-controlled corridors, XR security, autonomous maintenance.

Social impact
- Sharp inequality: "Theatre of Civilization" inside the crescent; abandonment, scavengers and danger outside.

Scene ideas
- Tourists arrive at GCT into a perfected illusion; beyond the wall, decay is visible but access restricted.
- UES used for diplomatic/branding events and art vaults rather than daily life.
