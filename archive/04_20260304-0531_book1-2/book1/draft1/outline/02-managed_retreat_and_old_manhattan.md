# Managed Retreat and "Old Manhattan"

Overview
- Instead of cinematic sinking, the city opts for a political, economic "managed retreat" and preserves a curated core: Old Manhattan.

Phases
- Denial & defenses: seawalls, pumps, retrofits.
- Economic tipping: insurance withdrawal, high maintenance costs.
- Vote & relocation: legal designation of Preservation Zone and relocation to New Manhattan.

Preservation model
- Limited, symbol-driven zone (Midtown, Grand Central, Central Park, UES crescent).
- Machines (AI + drones) maintain the zone; human presence minimized to hospitality, officials, caretakers.

Narrative angles
- The city ‘retires’ its body, preserving its brain as a museum/theme-park.
- Political theatre: framing the retreat as preservation rather than surrender.
