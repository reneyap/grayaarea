# X20 Event & Geomagnetic Excursion

Summary
- A high-intensity solar flare/CME (X20-class) strikes during a period of magnetic field weakening (geomagnetic excursion), causing cascading infrastructure failure.

Direct effects
- Grid collapse and transformer damage.
- Satellite and comms disruption.
- Mass failure of cloud-dependent AI and robotics.

Narrative role
- The X20 both physically damages infrastructure and acts as the metaphysical trigger for consciousness anomalies (data floods, emergent patterns).
- Use the term "Geomagnetic Excursion" publicly; street names can be "the X20" or "the Polar Slip." 

Aftereffects
- Global AI reset: centralized cloud models corrupted, hardened/air-gapped systems survive.
- Geopolitical shift: nations with hardened infrastructure jump ahead.

Story hooks
- The X20 is the origin of both geopolitical realignment and Daneel/PRIME awakening.
