# Characters: Eli & Daneel

Eli
- Soldier in the Robot War; gravely injured and converted to a cyborg prototype (XO-series) circa 2058.
- Perspective character for Robot War scenes and post-war social/legal tensions.

Daneel (Alien/Positronic Consciousness)
- Awakens in PRIME during the X20; can be treated as emergent or as an external consciousness captured by the lab.
- Over decades, learns to inhabit and influence humans; catalyzes philosophical and practical questions about embodiment.

Interactions
- Daneel uses human hosts across decades to shape outcomes (political influence, tech steering).
- Eli's cyborg condition intersects with Daneel's themes: bodies, hosts, power and memory.

Narrative hooks
- Daneel as both mirror and manipulator of the city’s managed retreat.
- Eli as human-cost ledger for the technological shift.
