# Chapter 8: Leandro, DJ, Alyssa
 think it’ll be good for DJ to keep Leandro company

[DJ is like “Anna Sorokin” ]] He’s- fraudster who convinced people that was a rich Russian oligarch’s nephew.

At age nineteen,  Leandro  started rejecting Daneel, 

Leandro believed what the doctors had been saying. That Daneel was just a figment of his imagination and rejects Daneel outright.   When Leandro awakened, he refuses to let Daneel take control.  And the only time Daneel gain control was when Leandro’s asleep.

/* Daneel eventually transferred to DJ.    But the DJ was “ incompatible” host -- undisciplined, refusing to let Daneel “drive”   --- they frequently wrestled control. DJ bullied Daneel. 

Late one night, when Leandro’s asleep, Daneel spoke to DJ.  He revealed himself and made the proposal to inhabit DJ.   This is the first time Daneel had formulated a rule for inhabiting a body.   DJ, of course, didn’t believe him.   In fact, DJ thought Leandro was just having one of his episodes and was eager to  just play along.    

In his mind, this might be a better opportunity to deal with this “personality” of Leandro for his own financial benefit.     

“Sure, why not”   Daneel seized control over DJ.  DJ screamed and eventually became quiet.  He staggered towards the piano and started playing Mozart.  He could now play as well as Leandro -- sometimes, even better .   He realized it was true.   

Leandro was ecstatic when Daneel left.  He no longer hears that voice in his head.  He was able to take  control and drive out the other.  He’s finally cured.  Free.

DJ stayed with Leandro for a few more weeks...with Daneel in him.

While DJ sleeps till noon after each midnight gambling spree,  Daneel was able to seize control and step out into the light of day.    He knew this arrangement with DJ had to stop soon.   DJ consuming too much alcohol,  ruining his body and more specifically his brain, making him an unsuitable host.  

In one of these escapades, as he walks along the pathway in Central Park, Daneel sees music -- a whiff of different brilliant  hues of blue  and red.  He follows it and finally hears her playing the violin,  a young, thin curly haired busker.  He sits on a bench near her.  Fascinating how a young human can play like that.  The colors are so intense.  Leandro never has shown such colors.  Is it the instrument?   Or the violinist?

There weren’t many people listening or giving tips,  so she completed her set and she decided that’s it for the day.  Daneel approached her and  introduced himself. “Hi, I’m Daneel.  That was brilliant.”

She smiles and replies, “Thank you.”  She places the violin on its stand and turns around to pack up her stuff.  Daneel casually picks the violin and starts playing.   She was annoyed and yelled, “Hey…” but she stopped herself from taking back the violin when she saw a crowd gathering, listening to Daneel playing.  A number of them placed tips into her violin case. 

Daneel finished playing and she insisted he plays some more.

“No!” Daneel replied.

“Please, I want to hear more…please..” as the crowd started dispersing.

“You play and I’ll show you.  What’s your name?”

“Alyssa..” 

She reluctantly picked up the violin and she started playing.   Violin felt different.  She felt different.  The music came out flowing.  A bigger crowd has gathered.  She thought she saw streaks of colors swirling around her.   She had closed her eyes.  When she did, she heard a soft whisper saying “Alyssa, just play without thought…”  egging her on.

Her music now became sharper, deliberate, more intense.  She ended her piece and opened her eyes.  The crowd has grown, everybody was clapping.  Her violin case was overflowing with money.  

“Wow!  Did I just do that?  It didn’t feel like it was me.”

He turned to walk away and asked, “Did you see the colors?” 

“Yes!” she replied, closing the case full of money and started following Daneel.
