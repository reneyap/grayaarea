## Eli  (14 years old)
“Whaea, when can we go out?  I’m bored.  There isn’t anything to do here,” Eli whined.

“Lije, we are still in quarantine.  We need to stay at home.  Until this outbreak is contained, we are not going out! Is that clear?!  ”

“But Wha…”


“This is not a discussion,”  Charlene said sternly.


Charlene returned to her study, leaving Eli with books.  Charlene didn’t allow Eli to waste his time binging  on screening devices or VR games.  Only books. Sometimes even physical books.


Eli eventually got bored. Ignoring his guardian, his biological grandmother, he ventured out.   He unlocked the internet connection and searched how to disable the tracking device strapped on his ankle.  A hacker offered to disabled the tracker for a few bit tokens.
