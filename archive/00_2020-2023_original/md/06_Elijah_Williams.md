## Elijah Williams 





*  *

Elijah Williams was  one of the lead special agents for the recently organized United East Pandemic Task Force,  an ex-marine and a former NYPD detective.  He had  just been assigned to the task force. 


Behind his back, he’d  been called derogatively as a Noth --Not Human.  He was  legally a human, with  a human brain, his birth DNA circulated in his body -- 1% organically human, the minimum requirement to be classified as human.   


In his last deployment to Egypt as a marine, he was severely injured.   Without his permission, he was transformed into a cyborg -- most of his body is a machine.  Immediately after the transformation, he was transferred to Marine Cyborg Logistics Command. 


He went AWOL to join the European Cyborg Resistance.  The resistance finally ended with the Global Declaration of Human Classification (GDHC).    Ultimately, he was “classified” as human but treated as one.  He joined the NYPD and became a detective.  His first partner, Rico, had his prejudices.   They eventually were able to earn each other's respect.  Unfortunately, after 5 years of partnership, his partner was killed on duty – in  a case involving  an underground cyborg organization.


Other detectives refused to partner with him.   He was eventually assigned a desk job for a couple months and at some point, he bring home a vigilante.  He was on his own to solve cases.  His captain had suspended him numerous times.  And he was reassigned to numerous other precincts. 


He was a loner -  a lone wolf.


There were many  times he could have been fired.  But he was a good cop. A great detective.   However, a number of people have attributed his “instinct” to his enhancements.   Eventually, he quit NYPD and joined the Federal Narcotics Agency as an undercover detective until he was extracted from his assignment when he was requested to be part of the task force.     

Initially he refused -- until the outbreak became a pandemic … in NYC.    There were rumors that it may be a biological weapon.  During the last war and subsequent rebellion he has been involved in the investigative team for biochemical weapons. 


***He was then adopted by his maternal grandmother, Charlene (......).when his unwed mother died giving birth to him.  He only knows Charlene as his Whaea, a Maori term for mother.***


He’d  never mentioned it to anyone.  But he was familiar with virology -  when he was growing up.His guardian, his biological grandmother,an immunological anthropologist, was  a professor at Columbia University.  When he was a teenager, he got obsessed with studying virology , after his Whaea died of encephalitis, an inflammation of the brain.  The doctors in New Zealand  informed him that the conditionwas caused by an unknown viral infection during one of those periodic outbreaks.


But he believed she was targeted- murdered - a victim of an artificial viral infection.  Since then he thought it could have been some sort of bio-weapon.  He remembered a stranger -- a teenager -- who was with her when she died.   But his memory is fuzzy.  He can’t remember the boy’s  face.  He couldn’t even remember her face anymore.  Even when he viewed  one of the video recordings of her, he knew  it was  his grandmother; but she was  becoming less recognizable.  Yes, it had  been at least 80 years since she died. The event seemed to be buried.  The doctors said he could be traumatized, he could be blocking it.  He was only 15 when it happened.
