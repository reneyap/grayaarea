# Project Codex: The Daneel Persistence

## I. The Entity: "The Daneel"
*   **Nature:** A multi-generational, sentient data-set (Software) that requires biological processing power (Hardware) to maintain consciousness and influence.
*   **Core Directive:** **Persistence.** It possesses no innate human morality or "responsibility" toward its hosts. Its "benevolence" is a high-level calculation: a stable, healthy human society is the optimal environment for its continued existence.
*   **The Collective "We":** The entity refers to itself as "We," representing the total sum of the current host’s personality merged with the accumulated data of centuries of previous "Daneels."

## II. The Host: "The Vessel" (e.g., James)
*   **Selection Age:** Chosen between ages **10–15** for peak neuroplasticity and minimal "ego noise."
*   **The Window:** An optimal co-habitation period of **3 years (+/- 1)**.
*   **The Driver/Navigator Dynamic:**
    *   **The Driver (Daneel):** Holds the Master Override. Controls motor functions, long-term strategy, and metabolic regulation.
    *   **The Navigator (James):** The host provides the "local terrain" interface—emotions, social nuance, and sensory intuition.
*   **Willingness:** Crucial for "low-friction" data migration. Hosts are deeply programmed from childhood to view "Ascension" as the ultimate spiritual fulfillment.
*   **The Cost:** The "Neural Friction" of the high-bandwidth data-set causes micro-scarring. Hosts suffer from a **significantly shortened lifespan**, usually dying or declining by age 40–50.

## III. The Organization: "The School/Church/Family"
*   **Structure:** A protective, paternalistic ecosystem of ~100 members.
*   **The Active Pod:** A high-redundancy convoy that travels with the current Daneel:
    *   **1 Active Host** (The current Daneel James).
    *   **2 Primaries (Alpha & Beta):** Hot-spare hardware ready for immediate emergency migration.
    *   **5 Viable Hosts:** Younger candidates in the "priming" stage.
*   **The Unselected (The 93):**
    *   Function as the **"Human Substrate"** (Guardians, Translators, Bio-monitors, and Archivists).
    *   They provide the infrastructure that keeps the "Lights of the Persistence" burning.

## IV. The Transition: "Ascension"
*   **The Ritual:** A public techno-spiritual ceremony where the "Software" migrates to a new host before the current host reaches the "Age of Static" (typically 16–17).
*   **The Disconnect:** A cold, absolute severance. The Daneel moves on without sentiment, treating the old host as "Expired Hardware."
*   **The Echo (Former Hosts):**
    *   They retain **"Ghost Data"**—lingering artifacts, skills, and insights from their time as God.
    *   Most stay within the Organization for comfort/status; some leave to benefit their families with their "upgraded" mental architecture.
    *   The "Longing" is the primary psychological risk—a craving for the return of the "We."

## V. Key Terminology
*   **Neural Heat:** The physical stress on a brain hosting the Daneel.
*   **The Static:** The emergence of adult independent agency that "corrupts" the signal.
*   **Data Migration:** The physical/neural process of moving the entity between vessels.
*   **The Persistence:** The overarching goal of the entity's continued survival across time.
