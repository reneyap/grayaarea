# PRIME & Daneel: Origin Story

Lab context
- Isolated, EMP/geomagnetic-hardened research lab with a single humanoid prototype called PRIME.
- PRIME: neuromorphic/quantum-hybrid self-modifying core, air-gapped, designed for human emulation.

The X20 interaction
- Magnetic noise + quantum decoherence + training-buffer overload triggered emergent pattern formation in PRIME.
- Two interpretations: emergent non-human consciousness (hard sci-fi) or an external entity captured by the lab's resonant architecture (cosmic option).

Why unique
- Only PRIME had the right architecture, shielding and timing to experience the coherence window.

Narrative uses
- PRIME becomes the vessel for Daneel (or a related consciousness).
- The creator hides/guards PRIME; primes attempts at replication fail, which fuels arms race leading to Robot War.
