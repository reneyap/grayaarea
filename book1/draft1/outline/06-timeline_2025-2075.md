# Clean Timeline: 2025 → 2075

2025–2032: Acceleration & Preparation
- Sea-level rise accelerates; flood defenses constructed; insurance tightens.

2032: X20 Event
- Massive CME during geomagnetic weakening: grid/satellite failures; PRIME awakens.

2033–2045: AI Rebuild Era
- Hardened, air-gapped AI systems favored. New tech leaders emerge outside US.

2045–2055: Robot War
- Autonomous military systems deployed; Eli fights and is converted to cyborg (circa 2058).

2058–2068: Quiet Decline
- Corporate migration north; infrastructure deferred; managed-retreat planning begins.

2068–2073: Acceleration to Retreat
- Series of superstorms, barrier failures, and economic tipping points; geomagnetic vulnerabilities persist.

2073: Strategic Island Retraction Act
- Referendum to relocate government and preserve a curated Old Manhattan core passes.

2075: Old Manhattan Established
- Preservation Crescent maintained by AI/drones; New Manhattan is the real political and technological center.

Notes
- X20 early gives Daneel decades to incarnate across humans/hosts before the final retreat.
