# Robot War & Geopolitical Shift

Post-X20 geopolitical landscape
- U.S.: centralized cloud-dependent AI crippled; slow recovery.
- China/EU/Russia: regions with hardened/distributed infrastructures gain advantage.

AI reset
- Cloud-model corruption; survivors are air-gapped, hardened stacks.
- New techno-oligarchs emerge (a "New Elon" likely non-US).

Robot War (2045–2055)
- Large-scale deployment of autonomous military systems leads to interstate kinetic conflict.
- Not "robot uprising" but state-level autonomous warfare and rogue firmware exports.

Eli and the war
- Eli fights in the Robot War, is gravely injured, and becomes an early cyborg prototype.
- Post-war social/legal frameworks (GDHC) create tensions around human/cyborg classification.

Narrative hooks
- Exported unstable firmware as a cause of localized catastrophes.
- Tech-magnates as new political players.
