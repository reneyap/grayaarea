## Part I: The Echo (2274)

### 00. Prologue: The Echo
*File:* `00_Prologue.md`
*   **Timeline:** Undefined / Metaphysical
*   **Summary:** A disembodied consciousness falls through the void, experiencing the terror of having no body, no senses, and no voice. The primal trauma of the "X20 Event."

### 01. Old Grand Central
*File:* `01_Old_Grand_Central.md`
*   **Timeline:** January 2274
*   **POV:** Eli Williams
*   **Setting:** Submerged Old Manhattan, Grand Central Museum.
*   **Plot:** 
    *   Eli leads a cyborg team to investigate a Stage-6 outbreak.
    *   Encounters infected "Twin" agents and a mysterious girl (Cleo).
    *   Eli is incapacitated by a high-frequency sonic attack ("The Hum").
    *   **Key Detail:** Eli has a built-in AI and CyComm module. He is a "Noth" (1% human).

### 02. Flashback: New Zealand
*File:* `02_Flashback_NZ_2274.md`
*   **Timeline:** September 2274 (Wait: This flashback seems to occur *after* the Grand Central incident or runs parallel? The text says "Sept 2274", while Ch 01 says "Jan". This is a continuity error to fix.)
*   **POV:** Daneel (as George Evans / Narrator)
*   **Plot:**
    *   Introduces **George Evans** (a Daneel host) investigating an outbreak in NZ.
    *   Introduces **Charlene Kapetaua** and young **Eli (14yo)**.
    *   Establishes the connection between Charlene and the virus/outbreak.
    *   **Lore:** Mentions the history of Daneel's transfers and the "Rules of Engagement."

### 03. Cleo, Saraswati, & Petrus
*File:* `03_Cleo_Saraswati_Petrus.md`
*   **Timeline:** 2274 (Post-Grand Central)
*   **POV:** Eli Williams
*   **Setting:** Field Hospital.
*   **Plot:**
    *   Eli finds Cleo dying.
    *   Cleo reveals herself as Daneel ("Call me Daneel").
    *   **The Transfer:** Daneel jumps from Cleo to **Petrus** (a bystander/boy).
    *   **Reveal:** Daneel knew Eli's grandmother and repeats her secret phrase.

## Part II: The Genesis (2063 – 2150s)

### 04. Origin: The X20 Event
*File:* `04_Origin_X20_Event.md`
*   **Timeline:** 2063
*   **POV:** Isaac Rubenstein
*   **Plot:**
    *   Isaac protects his home against the X20 Solar Flare.
    *   His android son, **Jason Prime**, malfunctions/awakens during the flare.
    *   A new consciousness (Daneel) is born in the static.

### 05. Call Me Daneel
*File:* `05_Call_Me_Daneel.md`
*   **Timeline:** 2063+
*   **POV:** Daneel
*   **Plot:**
    *   Daneel describes his early existence trapped in Jason Prime.
    *   Begins communicating with Isaac.
    *   Isaac names him **Daneel**.
    *   They form a partnership: Isaac creates the "Positronic Chamber" to study him.

### 06. The Rubensteins
*File:* `06_The_Rubensteins.md`
*   **Timeline:** Backstory (2023–2060)
*   **POV:** Third Person / Narrator
*   **Plot:**
    *   Isaac and **Alice's** history.
    *   The tragic death of their real son, **Jason**, to VR addiction.
    *   The origin of the "Jason Prime" android project.

### 07. The Tenant
*File:* `07_The_Tenant.md`
*   **Timeline:** 2060s
*   **POV:** Daneel
*   **Plot:**
    *   Daneel transfers *into* Isaac's mind.
    *   Lives as a "Tenant" (passive observer) for 6 years.
    *   Travels into the VR world ("Jason's Nook") at night.
    *   Isaac eventually dies of old age/illness.

### 08. The Glasses (Gap)
*File:* `08_The_Glasses.md`
*   **Status:** STUB / Incomplete
*   **Plot:** Daneel miniaturizes the Positronic Chamber into a pair of glasses to allow portable transfer.

### 09. The Coma Ward
*File:* `09_The_Coma_Ward.md`
*   **Timeline:** Post-Isaac
*   **POV:** Daneel
*   **Plot:**
    *   Daneel attempts to inhabit comatose patients.
    *   **Failure:** 15 patients reject him and die.
    *   Daneel is traumatized by the deaths he caused.

### 10. Christina & Leandro
*File:* `10_Christina_and_Leandro.md`
*   **Timeline:** ~2080s?
*   **POV:** Daneel
*   **Plot:**
    *   Daneel saves a fetus (**Leandro**) with Zika virus by inhabiting it.
    *   **Christina** (mother) is turned away from the hospital.
    *   Daneel heals Leandro from the inside.

### 11. Maurizio & Annabelle
*File:* `11_Maurizio_and_Annabelle.md`
*   **Timeline:** Leandro's Childhood
*   **Plot:**
    *   Christina is homeless.
    *   They are taken in by a kind couple, **Maurizio and Annabelle**.
    *   Daneel teaches Leandro piano and languages.

### 12. Leandro, DJ, & Alyssa
*File:* `12_Leandro_DJ_Alyssa.md`
*   **Timeline:** Leandro's Adulthood
*   **Plot:**
    *   Leandro becomes famous.
    *   **The Rejection:** At 19, Leandro rejects Daneel. Daneel leaves.
    *   Daneel inhabits **DJ** (a gambler) – a terrible host.
    *   Daneel meets **Alyssa** (street violinist).

### 13. Alyssa Visits Leandro
*File:* `13_Alyssa_visits_Leandro.md`
*   **Timeline:** Post-Transfer
*   **Plot:**
    *   Daneel (in Alyssa/DJ?) visits a dying Leandro.
    *   **The Beanie:** Leandro gives his beanie to Alyssa/Daneel.

### 14. Leaving Alyssa
*File:* `14_Leaving_Alyssa.md`
*   **Timeline:** Alyssa's 21st Birthday
*   **Plot:**
    *   Alyssa shows signs of neurological damage ("The Fade").
    *   Daneel leaves to save her life.
    *   **The Necklace:** Alyssa gives him her pendant.
    *   Daneel transfers to **Francis**.
