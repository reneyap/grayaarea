*   **Perspective:** Daneel

### 14_Chapter_9_Alyssa_visits_Leandro.md
*   **Timeline:** Post-Transfer
*   **Summary:** Daneel (in Alyssa?) visits a dying Leandro. Leandro gives his beanie to Daneel/Alyssa.
