# STORYLINE (Draft 0)

> **Status:** Current state of the narrative as scanned in `book1/draft0/chapters`.
> **Structure:** The draft currently alternates between two distinct timelines.

---

## Timeline A: The Pandemic (Old Manhattan, 2274)

### The Setup
*   **Eli Williams** is a cyborg detective (1% organic human) for the United East Pandemic Task Force. He has a history as a "Noth" (Not Human) and was raised by his grandmother, **Charlene Kapetaua** (Whaea).
*   **The Setting:** Old Manhattan is submerged/flooded. Grand Central Museum is the epicenter of a new, virulent outbreak (Stage-6 infection).

### The Incident
*   Eli leads a team of XR bots to Grand Central.
*   They encounter "The Twins" (Foundation agents) and a mysterious girl (**Cleo Saraswati**).
*   Eli is overwhelmed by a high-frequency "hum" (The Alien/Virus signal?) and passes out.
*   He wakes up outside, having been dragged to safety by Cleo.

### The Reveal
*   Eli tracks Cleo to a field hospital. She is dying of the infection.
*   Cleo speaks to Eli, calling him "Lije" (Isaac Asimov reference).
*   **The Transfer:** As Cleo dies, her consciousness/entity transfers to a young boy in the room, **Petrus**.
*   **Daneel:** The entity identifies himself as **Daneel**.
*   **The Connection:** Daneel reveals he knew Eli's grandmother, Charlene, 80 years ago. He repeats her dying words: *"Di mian kwa ee, wa ho seh"* (Hokkien for "Don't worry, I'm alright").
*   Daneel explains he has been transferring bodies for centuries.

## Timeline B: The Origins (2063 - 2150s)

### Generation 1: Isaac Rubenstein (The Creator)
*   **Backstory:** Isaac and Alice (scientists) lose their son, **Jason**, to a VR addiction/coma.
*   **The Vessel:** Isaac creates an android, **Jason Prime**, to preserve his son's memory.
*   **The Spark (2063):** An X20 Solar Flare hits. A disembodied consciousness (**Daneel**) awakens inside the android's data stream.
*   **The Tenant:** Daneel is trapped in the android. He eventually transfers into Isaac's mind, living as a passive observer ("The Tenant") in Isaac's brain for 6 years until Isaac's death.

### Generation 2: The Failures
*   **The Glasses:** Daneel modifies Isaac's glasses to facilitate consciousness transfer (The Positronic Glasses).
*   **Coma Ward:** Daneel attempts to inhabit 15 comatose patients. All reject him and die. Daneel is guilt-ridden.

### Generation 3: Leandro (The Artist)
*   **The Transfer:** A desperate Daneel transfers into a fetus inside a pregnant woman, **Christina**, who is turned away from the hospital.
*   **The Fix:** The fetus has Microcephaly (Zika). Daneel heals the brain structure from within.
*   **The Life:** Leandro is born. They are taken in by **Maurizio can Annabelle**.
*   **The Prodigy:** Daneel teaches Leandro languages and piano. Leandro becomes a world-class pianist.
*   **The Beanie:** Leandro wears a beanie to hide his slightly small head.
*   **The Departure:** At 19, Leandro rejects Daneel as a hallucination. Daneel is forced to leave.

### Generation 4: DJ (The Mistake)
*   Daneel inhabits **DJ**, a fraudster/gambler. It is a "hostile" tenancy. DJ is an unsuitable host.

### Generation 5: Alyssa (The Color of Music)
*   **The Meeting:** Daneel (in DJ) sees "colors" in the music of **Alyssa**, a street violinist.
*   **The Transfer:** Daneel transfers to Alyssa.
*   **The Symbiosis:** They perform together. Daneel enhances her musical ability (synesthesia).
*   **The Goodbye:** At age 21, Alyssa begins showing symptoms of neurological decay (the cost of long-term possession).
*   **The Gift:** Daneel leaves to save her. Alyssa gives him her **necklace**. Daneel transfers to a boy, **Francis**.

## Timeline Convergence (Fragmented / Stubbed)

The narrative jumps back to the 2274 timeline (or a convergence point) in the final chapters.

### The Elementals
*   Daneel realizes he has control over/allies in the "Elementals" (forces of nature, radio waves, gravity).

### The Climax (Grand Central / The Chamber)
*   **The Twins** serve as a channel to an "Alien" entity (the source of the virus/hum). They die from the strain.
*   **The Confrontation:** Eli, Daneel (in Petrus?), and the Alien connect via Eli's comlink.
*   **The Sacrifice:** The Alien reveals it is also an experiment/victim. It sacrifices itself or merges.
*   **The Merger:** To survive, Daneel might have jumped into Eli's body (implied). Or they coexist.
*   **The Rules:** Daneel establishes his "Three Rules of Engagement" for ethical possession:
    1.  Host must agree (Isaac's Rule).
    2.  Host must be in "Prime" state (Yellow Aura, ~14-18yo).
    3.  Host must be a willing passive observer (Don't Interrupt).

### The Artifacts of Succession
Daneel keeps three items to remember his humanity:
1.  **Isaac's Glasses** (Technology/Sight)
2.  **Leandro's Beanie** (Humility/Family)
3.  **Alyssa's Necklace** (Art/Love)
