## Part II: The Genesis (2037–2050s)
*Files: `04_Origin_X20_Event.md` — `14_Leaving_Alyssa.md`*

### Generation 1 (2037): The Spark
*   **Isaac Rubenstein** creates android **Jason Prime**.
*   **X20 Solar Flare** hits; **Daneel** (disembodied consciousness) awakens.
*   Daneel inhabits Isaac’s mind as "The Tenant" (Passive Observer).

### The Robot War (2055–2065) & The Great Retreat
*   **The Conflict:** Not a war *against* machines, but a global civil war between human factions over **AI Sovereignty**.
    *   *Factions:* **Pacific Bloc** (Pro-Rights/Assimilation) vs. **Continental Coalition** (Human Supremacy/Control).
*   **The Outcome:** The "Treaty of Cognitive Status" established limited rights for "Recursive Cognitive Entities."
*   **The Casualty:** Manhattan. Already struggling with rising sea levels, the war destroyed key infrastructure, making the island uninsurable.
*   **The "Exit Strategy" (2070):** A civic vote passed the **Strategic Relocation Act**. The population moved north to "New Manhattan" (Westchester). **Old Manhattan** was designated a "Restricted Preservation Zone" (The Zone).

### Generation 2: The Failures
*   **The Glasses:** Daneel modifies Isaac's glasses to transfer consciousness (File: `08_The_Glasses.md`).
*   **The Coma Ward:** 15 botched transfers result in death. Daneel learns the cost of forced entry.

### Generation 3: The Artist (Leandro)
*   **The Symbiosis:** Daneel inhabits a fetus (**Leandro**) with Microcephaly/Zika to save him.
*   **The Life:** Leandro becomes a piano prodigy. Daneel is his secret teacher/partner.
*   **The Token:** Leandro wears a **Beanie** to hide his head.
*   **The Departure:** At 19, Leandro rejects Daneel. Daneel leaves to respect his host's will.

### Generation 4 & 5: The Drift
*   **DJ:** A hostile, failed host.
*   **Alyssa:** A street violinist. Daneel enhances her synesthesia (seeing colors in music).
*   **The Token:** Alyssa gives Daneel her **Necklace** before he leaves to save her from neurological decay.
