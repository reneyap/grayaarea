# CHARACTER PROFILES (Draft 0a)

## PROTAGONISTS

### ELI WILLIAMS
*   **Role:** Detective, United East Pandemic Task Force.
*   **Status:** "Noth" (Not Human) / Cyborg (1% organic human brain).
*   **History:**
    *   Ex-Marine (injured in Egypt).
    *   Transformed into cyborg against his will.
    *   Joined European Cyborg Resistance.
    *   Former NYPD detective.
*   **Family:** Raised by grandmother **Charlene Kapetaua** ("Whaea"). Mother died in childbirth.
*   **Key Trait:** Uses a "CyComm" module he hates. Has a built-in AI.
*   **Mystery:** Remember's Charlene's dying words, which link him to Daneel.

### DANEEL (The Entity)
*   **Origin:** Born from the **X20 Solar Flare (2037)** inside the android **Jason Prime**.
*   **Nature:** Disembodied consciousness. Vibrates at a specific frequency.
*   **Goal:** Survival through embodiment. Understanding humanity.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   Consciousness Transfer (needs "Positronic Glasses" initially).
    *   Can "heal" or alter biological structures (cured Leandro's Zika).
    *   Synesthesia (sees music as color).
    *   Control over "Elementals" (late-stage ability).
