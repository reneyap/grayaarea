# WORLD LORE (Draft 0a)

## 2087 SETTING: THE PANDEMIC
*   **Location:** Old Manhattan (Retired/Restricted).
*   **Geography:** 
    *   **The Retreat:** Post-Robot War instability and rising seas led to the "Strategic Relocation Act" (2070).
    *   **The Zone:** South of 125th St is a "Historic Preservation Zone" (mostly abandoned, patrolled by drones). Lower Manhattan is partially submerged.
*   **The Foundation:** Secretive organization operating deep within the restricted zone. Claiming to "monitor stability," they actively select "Twins" as conduits.
*   **The Resonance Event (2087):**
    *   **Infection:** Stage-6 (Rapid onset, lethal).
    *   **Symptoms:** High-frequency sonic sensitivity ("The Hum"), seizures, then death.
    *   **Source:** Grand Central Museum (Epicenter).
*   **Cyborg Rights (GDHC):**
    *   **Legal:** "Global Declaration of Human Classification". Established after the Robot War to distinguish humans from "Recursive Cognitive Entities" (RCEs).
    *   **"Noth" (Not Human):** Derogatory slang for cyborgs.
    *   **Status 1%:** Minimum organic brain tissue required to be legally human.

## THE TECHNOLOGY
*   **Positronic Glasses:**
    *   Created by Isaac Rubenstein.
    *   Facilitate consciousness transfer into organic hosts without killing them (mostly).
    *   Look like ordinary glasses.
    *   Passed down from host to host.
*   **The Beanie:**
    *   Given to Leandro by Christina to hide his microcephaly.
    *   Passed down as a token of humility/family.
*   **The Necklace:**
    *   Given by Alyssa.
    *   See music as color (Synesthesia anchor?).
    *   Passed down as a token of emotion/art.
*   **CyComm Module:**
    *   New cybernetic implant Eli receives.
    *   Allows direct command/control of XR drones.
    *   Considered invasive by Eli.

## THE META-PHYSICS (2037+)
*   **X20 Event (2037):** Solar flare that awakened Daneel's consciousness.
*   **The Robot War (2055-2065):**
    *   Not AI vs. Human, but a global civil war between human factions over **AI Sovereignty**.
    *   Resulted in the **"Treaty of Cognitive Status"**: Limited rights for "Recursive Cognitive Entities" (RCEs).
*   **Consciousness Transfer:**
    *   Requires a "willing host" (ideally).
    *   **The Cost:** Long-term possession causes neurological decay (e.g., Alyssa's tremors).
    *   **Healing:** Can repair biological damage (e.g., Leandro's Zika brain).
*   **Synesthesia:** Daneel perceives music as color. This ability evolves from host to host.
*   **Elementals:** Late-stage ability to control/commune with forces of nature (gravity, radio waves).

## DANEEL'S RULES OF ENGAGEMENT
*   **Rule 1 (Isaac's Rule):** The host must **agree** to allow him to be an "active" consciousness. (Learned after the 15 failures).
*   **Rule 2 (Prime State):** Hosts should ideally be in their "prime" (~14-18 years old). Daneel perceives them as having a "brilliant yellow" aura.
*   **Rule 3 (Passive Observer):** The host must be willing to be a **passive observer** ("Don't Interrupt").
