Cleo Saraswati — Book 1 Host Profile (Canonical)

Identity
- Name: Cleo Saraswati
- Age: 17 (Book 1 opening)
- Affiliation: Foundation (missing for ~5 years before reappearance)
- Role: Daneel's primary host at Book 1 opening

Host Dynamics
- High pattern coherence and strong compatibility for Daneel persistence
- Cooperative and spiritually aware host presentation
- Speaks Hokkien phrase: "Di mian kwa ee, wa ho seh" ("Don't worry, I'm alright")
- Associated with pendant transfer event at hospital

Book 1 Arc Function
- Entry point for Eli's direct encounter with Daneel-in-host form
- Connects Eli's grandmother NZ Echo memory to present GCT outbreak
- Represents prepared/cooperative hosting state before catastrophic destabilization

Crisis State
- Exposed during GCT/Entity investigation
- Infection escalates to Stage-5 severity
- Becomes non-viable for long-term Daneel persistence
- Triggers emergency transfer to Petrus van Wyk

Canonical Notes
- Cleo is not framed as a permanent final host
- Cleo's collapse is the pivot from controlled cohabitation to survival transfer
- Leandro's beanie remains in her possession before Daneel reclaims it during transfer continuity
