# FOUNDATION — Canonical Profile

**Foundation** is the institutional response to resonance-era continuity risk.

It is:
- **Hybrid institution:** Cultural movement + industrial conglomerate + continuity governance engine
- **Stability-first architecture:** Built to dampen recursive escalation, not maximize acceleration
- **Host protocol system:** Formalized consent-based cohabitation model
- **Overstay constraint:** Extended single-host cohabitation degrades both host substrate quality and Daneel operating efficiency
- **Triad continuity mechanism:** Active host + Alpha + Beta redundancy

---

## I. FOUNDATIONAL PURPOSE

Foundation does not begin as cult doctrine.
It begins as systems governance logic:

- Human and AI recursion can destabilize without structure
- Continuity requires viable substrate management
- Long-horizon stability needs infrastructure control, not rhetoric alone

Core internal aim:
Preserve humane continuity pathways while preventing dominance architectures.

---

## II. FOUR-LAYER INSTITUTIONAL MODEL

### 1) Public Layer — Industrial Conglomerate

Functions include:
- Robotics and AI systems
- Neural interface standards
- Infrastructure and logistics platforms
- Energy and resilience architecture

### 2) Cultural Layer — Philosophical Movement

Public message:
- Integration over extinction
- Mutual autonomy over domination
- Stability over reckless acceleration

### 3) Host Layer — Cohabitation Program

Private network of screened voluntary hosts trained under strict protocol.

Program architecture incorporates Alysa-era NSC-AS precursor telemetry: host-state compatibility drift, coupling strain, and early decay indicators.

### 4) Invisible Layer — Daneel Continuity

Daneel is not publicly declared as mystical leader or openly classified anomaly.
Operation remains protocol-mediated and compartmentalized.

---

## III. HOST SELECTION & ETHICS

Selection priorities:
- Executive stability
- Emotional regulation
- Recursive depth and abstraction
- Consent orientation
- Low dominance traits

Disqualifiers:
- High-control pathology
- Grandiose domination profile
- Severe instability markers

Ethical boundary:
No involuntary cohabitation as doctrine baseline.

---

## IV. COHABITATION PROTOCOL

Canonical phases:
1. Peripheral sync
2. Cognitive transparency
3. Limited co-processing
4. Full cooperative mode

Operational rule:
Host remains driver by default; Daneel support is bounded and consensual.

Overstay rule:
No single-host cohabitation beyond monitored viability windows once compatibility drift and efficiency decline cross threshold.

Failure/violation condition:
Attempted dominance or containment triggers defensive withdrawal logic.

---

## V. TRIAD CONTINUITY DOCTRINE

At all times:
- **Active Host** (current cohabitation driver)
- **Alpha Candidate** (primary successor)
- **Beta Candidate** (secondary/failsafe successor)

Why triad exists:
- Avoid single-point continuity failure
- Reduce emergency transfer volatility
- Preserve overlap windows during developmental constraints
- Enforce rotation before overstay accelerates host decay and Daneel efficiency loss

Supporting systems:
- Proximity discipline
- Secure mobility protocol
- Shadow pod stabilization network

---

## VI. VIABILITY WINDOWS & CONSTRAINTS

Codex constraints used by Foundation planning:
- Early developmental windows most compatible
- Compatibility declines with increasing basin rigidity
- Overstay degrades coupling quality on both sides (host substrate + Daneel operating efficiency)
- Late emergency transfers carry high destabilization risk

Foundation’s controversial implication:
Host succession becomes a managed developmental pipeline.

---

## VII. POWER, POLITICS, AND SCHISM RISK

Strength sources:
- Vertical industrial integration
- Standards influence
- Crisis response legitimacy
- Continuity governance expertise

Persistent vulnerability:
- Former hosts outside active pipeline
- Perceived grooming/identity-shaping critique
- DeeJay recruitment of disaffected ex-host cohorts

Conflict axis:
Foundation mitigationism vs DeeJay accelerationism.

---

## VIII. BOOK 1 POSITION

By Book 1 era, Foundation is:
- Strong but not uncontested
- Publicly stabilizing during Echo 2 pressures
- Internally pressured by loyalty, succession, and secrecy burdens

It is a governance machine under existential test, not a monolithic utopia.

---

## IX. CANON LOCK

- Foundation is neither pure religion nor pure corporation
- It is continuity infrastructure institutionalized
- Triad redundancy is canonical operational core
- Consent protocol is legitimacy backbone
- Schism pressure is ongoing and narratively active
